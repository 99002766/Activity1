About this resort

            Set 20 km from Mysore Junction train station, this upscale resort surrounded 
            by greenery is also 7 km from Hebbal Lake and 12 km from Mysore Palace.

            Elegant rooms come with free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs. Villas add balconies
            and living rooms, as well as garden views.

            A warm restaurant with wood-panelled walls serves international cuisine.There's
            also a cocktail lounge and a coffee shop. Other amenities include a gym, a steam
            room, Ayurvedic treatments, and a pool with a sundeck, plus a beauty salon, and 
            banquet and conference facilities.
